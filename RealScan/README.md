RealScan is an AI provenance detector that analyzes text and images to determine if they were AI-generated. It looks for micro-signals, structural patterns, and artifacts to provide a likelihood percentage, confidence score, and detailed breakdown of its findings.

## Usage Note
**Important:** It may take more than one scan to get an accurate analysis. AI detection is not an exact science, and running the analysis multiple times or on different segments of the content can yield a more comprehensive understanding.
